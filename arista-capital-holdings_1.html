FAQ

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about our services, processes, and requirements.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is always ready to help you directly.

Ask via WhatsApp →
What documents are required to apply for financing?
+
You will need a valid national ID (KTP), proof of income (salary slip or business financials), a recent utility bill, and relevant collateral documents depending on the type of financing.
How long does the application approval process take?
+
Our standard approval process takes 1–3 business days once all documents are submitted. For eligible applicants, we offer an expedited 24-hour approval track for select financing products.
What are the minimum and maximum financing amounts?
+
Vehicle and electronic financing start from Rp 5,000,000, while business capital financing ranges from Rp 50,000,000 up to Rp 5,000,000,000 depending on eligibility and collateral.
Are there any early settlement fees?
+
Arista Capital Holdings does not charge early settlement penalties after the 6th month of your financing term. We believe in giving our clients full flexibility to manage their finances.
Is Arista Capital Holdings licensed and regulated?
+
Yes. Arista Capital Holdings operates under the full regulatory framework of Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK), ensuring complete compliance with Indonesian financial laws and maximum client protection.
Can I apply if I have an existing loan elsewhere?
+
Having an existing loan does not automatically disqualify you. Our credit team evaluates each application holistically, considering your total financial position and repayment capacity.
